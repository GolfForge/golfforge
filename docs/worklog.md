## 2026-06-01 — GOL-109 putting model: stimp-aware friction on the range (Windows)

- **One-line core fix:** `Physics/GroundRoll.{h,cpp}` got `GolfBallFlight::PutterSurfaceRoll(double StimpFt)` — returns an `FSurfaceRoll` with friction = `0.67 / StimpFt` (stimpmeter math: stimp reading is rollout in feet at the standard ~2 m/s release), bounce + spin terms zeroed. Default stimp 11 ft → friction 0.061 → 4 m/s rolls 13.4m ≈ 44 ft. Augusta-fast stimp 14 → 0.048 → ~56 ft.

The bounce loop self-skips (Vv ~ 0 from the near-flat putt input), so the trajectory is roll-only. Final lie still comes from the splatmap classifier — only friction is overridden.

## 2026-06-01 — GOL-110 trajectory Z tracks terrain (Windows)

- **`Physics/BallRender.{h,cpp}` — pure-C++ helper.** Two free functions: `CachePostLandingGroundZ` walks `Trajectory.Samples[LandingSampleIndex..end]`, calls a `TFunctionRef<TOptional<float>(double X, double Y)>` provider for each sample's world XY, and stores the result (or a `NoGroundZUU` sentinel). `SampleToWorld` then maps a sample into world UU, applying the snap when terrain info is available. Headless-testable; AGolfBallActor wraps it with a `LineTraceSingleByChannel(bTraceComplex=true, ignore pawn)` provider.
- **`AGolfBallActor::PlayTrajectory` pre-traces post-landing samples** and caches per-sample ground Z (`PostLandingGroundCacheUU`). `SampleToWorld` now takes the sample index; for post-landing samples it returns `cached + BallRestHeightUU + Sample.PositionMeters.Z * MtoUU` — preserves the bounce arc while tracking terrain underneath. For flight samples it **lerps the Z baseline from `LaunchOriginUU.Z` toward `cache[0] + BallRestHeightUU` over the flight time** — this is the critical bit: without it, the touchdown transition is a multi-meter vertical teleport whenever launch terrain Z != landing terrain Z, which reads as the ball "going through the floor" on the first bounce. With it, the whole trajectory is continuous.
- **`golfsim.FireShot::GetOrSpawnBall` traces launch origin to floor** (`bTraceComplex=true`, ignore pawn). Mirrors the range HUD recipe (`11fc1d7`). Covers the spawn case independently of in-flight Z snap.
- **`BallRestHeightUU` promoted** from `GolfRangeHUD.cpp`'s file-local to `AGolfBallActor::BallRestHeightUU` (public `static constexpr`). Single source of truth shared by the range HUD's launch trace, `golfsim.FireShot`'s launch trace, and the terrain snap.
- **Verification.** 34/34 Golfsim automation tests pass (3 new `Golfsim.BallRender.{FlatGround, SteppedTerrain, NoGroundFallback}` + 31 existing). MCP-fired `golfsim.FireShot 55 16 0 7000 0` on `GolfForgeDemoBlack` from world origin: ball arcs through flight and lands flush on the visible turf, no teleport into the floor at touchdown. User-verified in PIE.

## 2026-06-01 — GOL-38 ground-roll: multi-bounce / skip model (Windows)

- **Replaced GOL-9's flat friction-slide with an analytical bounce phase + roll continuation.** `Physics/GroundRoll.cpp::SimulateGroundRoll` now: (1) applies the descent + spin attenuation once as the initial landing scrape on Vh; (2) loops up to 6 parabolic hops, each launched by `Restitution * VvDown` upward and `Vh *= BounceHorizontalKeep` per bounce, stopping when `VvOut < 0.40 m/s`; (3) continues with the existing constant-friction roll from whatever Vh remains. Hop parabolas are sampled (~`2*Vv/g` per hop, 4-30 samples) into `RollSamples` with the correct Z so the ball visibly arcs in playback (`GolfBallActor::SampleToWorld` already interpolates Z — no consumer change needed).
- **`FSurfaceRoll` field reinterpretation:** `Restitution` is now the **vertical coefficient of restitution per bounce** (was: horizontal-speed retention into roll); new field **`BounceHorizontalKeep`** carries the per-bounce horizontal decay. `RollFriction` and `SpinCheck` unchanged.
